Services

Skypoint Farm is a full service Hunter Jumper equestrian facility providing boarding, lessons, training, and sales.

Lessons

At Skypoint Farm, we focus on the development of a rider's fundamentals, emphasizing form and function.

Boarding

We offer premium full-care boarding with 24/7 monitoring and on-site management.

Training

Our experienced and nationally recognized trainers are dedicated to the development of horse and rider.

IEA & IHSA Teams

We are home to the Skypoint IEA and Kent State IHSA Hunt Seat teams.

Sales & Leases

We pride ourselves in matching horses and riders, forming happy, lasting partnerships. We have the resources to help you find your perfect match.

Showing

We compete at rated and schooling shows year-round.
